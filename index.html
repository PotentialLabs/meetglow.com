--- title: Glow layout: page ---

Glow is a smart energy tracker for the home. It gives users real-time feedback about their energy use leading to energy savings and reduced impact on the environment. Glow’s breakthrough inductive wireless sensor allows for anyone to install it in just a few minutes. The device will also alert users about extended high usage that could be dangerous and provide simple on-the-go energy monitoring and budgeting tools from the companion mobile app.
