Home decor meets rocket science

Glow’s stylish in-home unit provides visual feedback. Meanwhile, a breakthrough sensor monitors your home’s electricity usage without any wiring.

  • Smart energy indicator glows to show energy usage
  • Ambient light sensor adjusts brightness at night
  • Solid wood base (optional) provides a stylish home accent
  • Patented sensor attaches to your meter in seconds
  • Magneto-resistive technology wirelessly measures power usage
  • Low-power radio sends data into your home for processing
