Meet Glow

Glow is a smart energy tracker for the home. It gives users real-time feedback about their energy use leading to energy savings and reduced impact on the environment. Glow’s breakthrough inductive wireless sensor allows for anyone to install it in just a few minutes. The device will also alert users about extended high usage that could be dangerous and provide simple on-the-go energy monitoring and budgeting tools from the companion mobile app.

Who we are

Potential Labs is a small startup nestled in the hills of Southeast Ohio and part of the Appalachian region. We strive to build solutions that help today’s consumers quantify their use of resources with the goal of reducing waste. Reducing waste saves users money and provides an important reduction of a home’s impact on the environment.

Smart Energy Indicator

Glow’s energy indicator adapts to your home and changes color in real time to help you understand your energy use.

High Usage Warning

Left the coffee pot or oven on? The fridge door open? If Glow notices something unusual it’ll send you a friendly notification so you can investigate.

Simple Monthly Budget

Set how much you want to pay each month and get an easy way to track your progress.

Auto-dimming light sensor

Glow includes an ambient light sensor and automatically dims its indicator at night or in other low-light conditions.
